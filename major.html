A System To Filter Unwanted Messages from OSN User Walls

Abtract

One fundamental issue in today's Online Social Networks (OSNs) is to give users
the ability to control the messages posted on their own private space
to avoid that unwanted content is displayed. Up to now, OSNs provide little support to this requirement.
To fill the gap, in this paper, we propose a system allowing OSN users to have a direct control on the messages posted on their walls.
