Peripheral Trouble Shooting And Management

Abtract

In Peripheral trouble shooting and management we implement an online complaint
posting which were posted by user about the trouble shoot that has occurred in the user peripherals.
The Admin who assigns the work to the engineers checks the pending complaints of the users.
